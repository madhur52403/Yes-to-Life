ABOUT



The human mind is an ancient being and we live in an ever-changing high-tech world. Our minds are associated with linear growth and fail to keep pace with the exponential rise in technology. Consequently, we are stuck in-between, perhaps our brains are. People are addicted to smartphones/screens in general. From liking a post on FB, to tweeting about your favourite celebrity, to posting a selfie on Insta, to snapping your life on Snapchat, etc we are stuck in the ever amplying feedback loop of addiction(without even realizing a thing). Enhancing your capabilitesEvery like, post, double-tap, notification-check, message from someone, etc are actually what are known as "Rewarding Stimuli" which triggers neurochemical release inside of our brains specifically, DOPAMINE. So, without even knowing we are already addicted to these reward and hapiness giving chemicals, no wonder we don't leave our phones idle even for an hour a day.

With this website SÍ A LA VIDA we aim at transforming the habits of people for better mental health and well being. We'll be providing several daily basic tasks that will help people to step aside from the tech-driven world and help them in connecting to the nature, appreciating its beauty, appreciating their own lives, feel good and proud about themselves, learn a bit about human brain, etc. The bottom line is we want you to leave your smartphones alone for some time in a day and indulge in these tasks and help your brain to get out of this addiction loop. Moreover with every completed task you will be awarded certain Points/Tokens which may then be used to grab you some Badges so that you can flaunt your progress with the world. Come join us in this journey to train your mind and calm your spirit.
